Global Warming and Climate Change

Polar bears are threatend by melting sea ice caused by global warming and climate change. Sea ice is an important resource for them to eat, hunt, find mates, and teach their cubs how to live. Lack of this essential sea ice leads to their population decrease in numerous areas. Let's find out more.
